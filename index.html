Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Early education focuses on foundational skills: language, math, motor skills, creativity, social interaction, exploration.

Elementary School

Elementary school curriculum emphasizes literacy, math, science, social studies, art, physical education, and critical thinking.

Middle (10-16) Years

Middle school curriculum focuses on advanced subjects: math, science, language arts, social studies, and electives.
